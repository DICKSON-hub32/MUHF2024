About MksU Hackfest 2024

The Machakos University (MksU) Tech and Engineering Community presents the second MksU Hackfest, focused on "Sustainability in Engineering and Technology Solutions." In partnership with the MksU Innovation Club, Microsoft Learn Students Ambassadors, Google Developer Student Club Machakos University, and Techo-Engineering Association, we offer a platform for students from diverse backgrounds to develop sustainable solutions for real-world challenges.

This dynamic event fosters innovation through the convergence of minds and perspectives. Participants will enhance skills via workshops and mentorships centered on sustainable Technological and Engineering practices, bridging academia and industry.

After a successful inaugural edition, join us again to shape a sustainable future. Be part of something greater, driving positive change through your creativity. MksU Hackfest 2.0 calls for innovation, collaboration, and empowerment.
